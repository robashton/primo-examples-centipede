Centipede 2013

Exposition

You are the villain, a pesky centipede found in the garden!

A villain you say? Why's that you say? Eating the flowers I say!

Thankfully the garden is protected by a noble defender who has his own agenda of destruction

Avoid the bullets, collect the flowers - and be fast, or you'll be left with no stone left to hide behind

Controls

Arrow keys if you're on a PC

Swipe in a direction if you're on a mobile device
